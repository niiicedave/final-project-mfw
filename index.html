Monterey Adventure

The land of John Steinbeck, Old Monterey and Cannery Row, Pebble Beach and 17-Mile Drive, Point Lobos, Big Sur and Highway 1, Arroyo Seco, and Mission San Antonio de Padua and the missions of Central California. Any road trip here is surely the end of all road trips! Miles of road wander through the heart of the former Spanish colony of California.

Earthquake Country!

Santa Cruz County

Gilroy, Henry W. Coe, Nisene Marks, Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk, Henry Cowell Redwoods, Big Basin Redwoods, Seacliff Beach.
